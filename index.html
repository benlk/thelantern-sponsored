Sponsored tweet tracker for @TheLantern

In this table's calculation of the ratio of ad to non-ad tweets, the number of ads is the number of tweets since the first sponsored tweet that are marked as ads by a subsequent tweet identifying the tweet as an ad. The number of non-ad tweets is the sum of retweets and other tweets.

The current ratio is ad tweets per content tweet.

Last updated 2015-09-13 at 11:00 a.m. Eastern time.
